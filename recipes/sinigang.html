Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Sinigang soup base
  • 1 lb pork, beef, or chicken
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 radish, sliced in circles
  • 1 cup spinach
  • meat seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil 1 litre of salted water in a pot on high
  2. Add meat to boiling water, let simmer on low-medium for 10 minutes
  3. Add vegetables, cover pot
  4. Let cook for 5 minutes
  5. Serve with rice
