Ground Beef

This page will help you cook ground beef through browning, which is perfect for tacos, burritos, and other similar dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb medium groun beef
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • Steak seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Chili pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Heat lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
  2. Place ground beef in pan, and cover pan with beef in one layer
  3. Let beef brown for 5 minutes, do not touch the beef during this time
  4. Add seasonings to taste
  5. Turn down to low-medium heat
  6. Using a wooden spoon, break up ground beef into smaller chunks every muinute, avoiding stirring
  7. When beef is appropriately browned and moisture is drained, remove from heat
  8. Serve
