Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 whole russet potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Seasonings
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil salted water on high heat
  2. Add potatoes to water, let boil for 15 minutes
  3. In a separate pan, heat butter and milk until they mix
  4. remove potatoes from water, drain pot
  5. Replace the potatoes in the pot, adding milk/butter mixture.
  6. Over low heat, mash the potatoes with a masher until desired texture is reached
  7. Remove from pot, cool, and serve
